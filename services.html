How do I get started with Jennavi?
Book a free 30-minute strategy call on Calendly. Jennifer reviews your LinkedIn before the call and arrives with specific observations. After the call, if it is a fit, you receive a proposal and can start within the week.
Will the content actually sound like me?
Yes. Jennifer spends significant time in the onboarding process learning your voice, tone, stories and specific expertise. Every post is written to sound like you on your best day, not like a ghostwriter.

Yes. Every client signs a service agreement before work begins covering scope, deliverables, revisions, payment terms and confidentiality. This protects both parties and ensures full clarity on what is being delivered.
How quickly will I see results?
Most clients see meaningful impression growth and profile visit increases within the first 30 days. Consistent inbound inquiries typically develop between month 2 and month 4 as LinkedIn learns your positioning and distributes your content to the right audience.
